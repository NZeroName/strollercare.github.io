FAQs

What services do you offer?

We offer two options: on-site cleaning and a free pick-up and drop-off service.

Do I need to prepare anything before the cleaning?

No preparation is needed. For on-site service, just schedule your appointment, and weâ€™ll handle the rest. If you choose our pick-up and drop-off service, please ensure the items are ready for collection on the scheduled day.

How long does the cleaning process take?

The duration depends on the type and condition of the item, but typically it takes around 30-60 minutes. This timing applies to both on-site and pick-up/drop-off services.

Are the cleaning products safe for my baby?

Yes, we use eco-friendly and baby-safe cleaning products for both on-site and pick-up/drop-off services.

Do you guarantee proper cleaning and sanitizing?

Yes, we guarantee that our cleaning and steam sanitizing process thoroughly removes dirt, stains, and bacteria. If you are not satisfied with the results, please let us know, and we will address any concerns.

How should I clean my baby gear between professional cleanings?

To maintain your baby gear between our professional cleanings, regularly wipe down surfaces with a damp cloth, use baby-safe cleaning products, and avoid harsh chemicals. For deeper cleaning, consider scheduling regular appointments with us.

Do you provide repair services for baby gear?

No, we do not offer repair services. Our focus is on cleaning and sanitizing your baby gear. For repairs, we recommend contacting the manufacturer or a specialized repair service.
